What this is

Chaos is expensive.
Systems make growth
repeatable.

Systems Studio Workshop is an operations and systems consulting practice for service-based businesses that have outgrown their current infrastructure — or never built one to begin with.

I enter where things feel heavy: scattered leads, manual follow-up, no clear process, founders carrying the business entirely in their heads. I map what's actually happening, identify where the friction is, and build lightweight systems that make the business easier to manage day to day.

Every engagement starts with a diagnostic. Every diagnostic produces a written assessment — regardless of whether you continue. The value is immediate. The build is optional.

Systems are customized using tools like Airtable, Notion, Google Workspace, and automation platforms when needed. No overly complex tech stacks. Designed for real business owners, not tech teams.

"The goal is not more tools.
The goal is a business that feels easier to run."
Fewer missed follow-ups Clearer client tracking Organized workflows Centralized information Easier onboarding Less mental clutter Better operational visibility
