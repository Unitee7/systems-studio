What this is

The business
should work as well
as you do.

Systems Studio Workshop is a consulting practice for service-based small businesses and solo operators — single owners and small teams up to six people — who are good at what they do but overwhelmed by everything around it.

I come in to help fix the gaps that keep your business from flowing — from missed leads and unclear handoffs to inconsistent client experiences and daily tasks that depend too much on you.

Every engagement starts with a diagnostic. Every diagnostic produces a written assessment — regardless of whether you continue. The value is immediate. The build is optional.

I use tools like Notion, Airtable, Google Workspace, Tally, Mailchimp, and Zapier when needed. No complex tech stacks. Designed for real business owners, not tech teams.
